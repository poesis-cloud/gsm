## Becoming a maintainer

Sustained, high-quality contribution (specification, conformance, reviews, or implementations) earns nomination. Maintainers are added by maintainer supermajority per `GOVERNANCE.md` §4–§5. Contribution terms (DCO + CLA) are in `CONTRIBUTING.md`.
