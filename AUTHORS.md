# Authors & Origin

The **Generative System Model (GSM)** is a **Poesis** standard
(<https://github.com/poesis-cloud>) — published, stewarded, and credited under the **Poesis**
name. This file is the canonical **origin record** for GSM.

## Authoring entity / origin

- **Poesis** — author, steward, and origin of the GSM standard. Economic copyright in the
  specification and reference code is held by Poesis (currently by the founder, pending
  incorporation of the Poesis legal entity, after which it is assigned to the entity — see the
  GSM strategy plan).

## Creator

- **Clément Cazaud** (founder, Poesis) — the individual who conceived and principally wrote
  GSM: the *Generative System Model*, the *DNA governance grammar* (Directive / Norm /
  Ascription), *Ascription* and *Archetype* typing, and the eight-primitive core. Named as
  creator; this authorship credit is personal and durable, independent of the economic
  copyright held by Poesis.
